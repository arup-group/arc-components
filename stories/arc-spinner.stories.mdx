# ArcSpinner
### `<arc-spinner>`

The ArcSpinner is used to show the progress of an indeterminate operation.

<Canvas>
  <Story name="Default" story={stories.Default} />
</Canvas>

## Examples
### Colors
The spinner's colors can be changed by setting the --stroke-color custom property.
<Canvas>
  <Story name='RedColor' story={stories.RedColor} />
  <Story name='GreenColor' story={stories.GreenColor} />
  <Story name='BlueColor' story={stories.BlueColor} />
  <Story name='PurpleColor' story={stories.PurpleColor} />
</Canvas>

### Sizing
Spinners are sized based on the current font size.
To change their size, set the font-size property on the spinner itself or on a parent element.

  <Story name='Small' story={stories.Small} />
  <Story name='XXLarge' story={stories.XXLarge} />
  <Story name='XXXXLarge' story={stories.XXXXLarge} />
</Canvas>
